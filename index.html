Jaundice

Ayurvedic name: Yakrit vriddhi

Don'ts (Apathyas)

  • Fat-rich, heavy diet
  • Food with pesticide contamination
  • Excessive consumption of alcohol
  • Daytime sleep

Do's (Pathya)

  • Patient may be kept on a restricted diet for a few days
  • Food consisting of Sali variety of rice, Yava (Barley), Mudga (Munga dal)
  • Cow milk, buttermilk

Curative Herbs

  • Katuki (Picrorhiza kurro)
  • Bhumyamalaki (Phyllanthus amarus)
